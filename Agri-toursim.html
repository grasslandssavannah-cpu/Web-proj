Agri-tourism: Cultivating Heritage

The Sustainable Savanna Experience

INTRODUCTION: Our Agri-tourism expedition is a curated journey connecting you directly with the **sustainable farming communities** that thrive on the savanna's edge. This isn't just a tour; it's a deep dive into local economies, traditional agricultural techniques, and the ethical harvesting practices that sustain the region. We offer a **professional, immersive, and educational** experience suitable for individuals and specialized groups.

What You Can Expect

  • Guided by **Agricultural Scientists and Local Elders** for authentic insight.
  • Hands-on participation in planting, harvesting, or processing activities.
  • Exclusive visits to innovative **water management** and **soil conservation** projects.
  • Traditional meals prepared with ingredients harvested that day.

Sample Itinerary Highlights (6 Days)

The standard six-day tour is designed to showcase the full cycle of life on the savanna farm:

  • **Day 1-2:** Arrive at the private cooperative; introduction to **organic crop rotation** and animal husbandry.
  • **Day 3:** Field day focusing on **irrigation systems** and water conservation techniques.
  • **Day 4:** Cultural exchange and cooking workshop with the host community.
  • **Day 5:** Exploration of a local **coffee or tea plantation** and processing facility.
  • **Day 6:** Farewell breakfast and transfer.

Required Skill/Fitness Level

This expedition is **Low to Moderate** intensity. It requires a willingness to participate in light farm work (walking, light lifting) and respect for the local community's culture and customs.
